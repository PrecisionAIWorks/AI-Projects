Our Solution: A Clear Path to Effective Communication

We need clear communication guidelines that define which channels to use based on urgency, complexity, and the need for traceability. For example: use Slack for quick async updates or coordination, email for formal summaries or non-urgent external communication, meetings for complex decision-making, and project trackers or docs for anything that must be referenced later. Publish a simple channel usage guide and reinforce it through onboarding and team rituals. This will reduce noise, increase clarity, and ensure important information reaches the right people at the right time‚Äîand stays findable.
